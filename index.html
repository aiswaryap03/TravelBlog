Phuket & Krabi, Thailand
By Jeena Kuas on July 20, 2021

Nestled in the Andaman Sea, Phuket and Krabi are twin jewels of southern Thailand that captivate travelers with their exotic charm and natural splendor. Phuket, the larger and more developed of the two, offers a dynamic mix of vibrant nightlife, bustling markets, luxurious beach resorts, and golden sands stretching along Patong, Kata, and Karon beaches. Just a short boat ride away, Krabi unveils a more tranquil side of paradise.
